About Me

Hello everybody, welcome to my brand new portfolio! Building this web page was one of the first homework assignments that I had to complete as part of the University of Texas Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp. I must admit, it wasn't easy! But, I definitely learned a lot and I also realize that I have so much more to learn. Astonishment doesn't even begin to describe how I feel about the absolute vastness of the ever changing landscape of web development technologies.

You may be wondering what brought me to sign up for this fast paced onslaught of information. Well, the reasons that I enrolled are the same as when I enrolled to Texas State University where I completed a 4 year degree in environmental sciences. I did it because I am a curious human and have a desire to pursue new experiences and learn new skills. As an Envrionmental Specialist with the City of Austin and over 5 years of experience in the environmental industry I continue to grow. But, as always, the new and unknown piqued my curiousity. Web development is something very unknown to me and I believe that's what makes it so attractive. At the moment of writing this, the path looks very daunting and I can feel the pressure rising. However, along with those feelings also comes utter excitement! When I began this journey I immediately felt relieved to be embarking on a new adventure and challenge. It was as if something was amiss in my life and I finally just discovered it. And so, I continue to push forward, inching closer and closer to the precipice of no return.

With the skills gained in this course I aim to launch an exciting new career as a full stack web developer. Please take the time to visit my portfolio page to see the projects that I have personally developed and deployed. Also, feel free to reach out with any questions, comments or ideas. I am happy to connect and welcome collaboration. Cheers!
